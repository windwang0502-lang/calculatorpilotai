## Contact Page Improvements

### Issue: Generic Form Wording
The contact page had template-style wording that felt impersonal:

**Before:**
> "We would love to hear from you. Whether you have found a bug, want to suggest a new tool, or simply have a question — send us a message and we will get back to you as soon as possible."

**After:**
> "Have a question, found a bug, or want to suggest a new tool? We would love to hear from you. We typically respond within 24–48 business hours."

**Changes:**
- More concise opening
- Clear response time expectation
- Professional SaaS tone

### Issue: Response Time Inconsistency
Response times were inconsistent between sections.

**Before:**
- Email card: "1–2 business days"
- Success message: "1–2 business days"

**After:**
- Email card: "We typically respond within 24–48 business hours"
- Success card: "within 24–48 business hours"
- Added "Support Hours: Monday–Friday, 9am–5pm EST"

### Issue: Success Message Tone
**Before:**
> "Thank you for reaching out. We have received your message..."

**After:**
> "Thanks for reaching out. We have received your message..."

**Changes:**
- More natural, less formal
- Removed unnecessary "Thank you" duplication
