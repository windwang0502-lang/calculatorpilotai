- **Finance** is broad but heavily mortgage/state-variant weighted.

- **Time** covers foundational date/time math, but payroll/scheduling/planning long-tail is sparse.

### High-opportunity gap themes

3. **AI**: tokenization, throughput, context/cost optimization variants, enterprise usage planning.

5. **Time**: work/payroll/shift/overtime/business-date variants and countdown/event planning.

2. **Audience variants**
   - Example: student/freelancer/self-employed/retiree-specific calculator pages.
3. **Scenario variants**
   - Example: “with extra payments”, “weekly vs monthly”, “high inflation”, “low utilization”.
4. **Carrier/provider variants**
   - Example: provider-specific shipping/AI pricing pages with distinct assumptions.
5. **Conversion utility variants**
   - Example: token/word/character/time/unit conversion pages grouped by workflow.

Long-tail constraints for quality:
- Unique intro + assumptions + FAQ per page
- Distinct formula notes or examples per variant
- Related calculators and guides mapped at generation time
- Canonical discipline and no near-duplicate slug collisions
